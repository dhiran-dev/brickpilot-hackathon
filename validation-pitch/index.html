The Pre-Dev Trap

Early residential decisions are often made from disconnected sketches, inspiration images and rough cost guesses.

The hard question arrives later: can the chosen idea actually fit the site, program, circulation and vertical constraints together?

Traditional Workflow
  • One-off sketches with no canonical geometry
  • Inspiration images detached from the plan
  • Feasibility checked after a direction is chosen
  • Hard constraints discovered late
BrickPilot Workflow
  • Versioned brief and canonical geometry
  • Distinct, constraint-aware scheme directions
  • Plan, massing and render evidence stay linked
  • Decision-ready feasibility package

07 // VALIDATION ENGINE

Automated Verification

BrickPilot promotes only a scheme that passes its deterministic geometry, circulation, architectural-intent and physical-evidence checks.

The gate is versioned and explainable: findings name the failed rule, the objects involved and the next useful action. It is feasibility evidence, not permit or structural approval.

Geometry & Access

Canonical regions, openings, envelope containment, reachability and protected circulation.

Intent & Physical Form

Main entry, roof geometry, supports, guards and facade intent are checked against the built form.

Scheme Set & Trade-offs

Near-duplicate schemes are rejected; bounded preference relaxations are surfaced instead of hidden.

Hard Gates

Versioned & explainable

A Modern, Grounded Stack

BrickPilot combines deterministic spatial modeling with production web infrastructure and carefully bounded AI services.

Each component has a clear role: capture the brief, preserve canonical data, inspect the form, and create reviewable concept evidence.

Application

Next.js + React for the interactive product workspace.

Data

Drizzle ORM + PostgreSQL for versioned studies and design evidence.

Spatial

Three.js for inspectable 3D massing and camera references.

Reasoning

Fireworks AI for structured LLM intake and advisory review.

Generative Renders

Replicate + GPT Image 2, grounded by the selected massing reference.

Artifacts

React PDF turns the selected study into a shareable feasibility package.
